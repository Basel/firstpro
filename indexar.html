About me

I'm second year ICT student, majoring Business IT & Mangement at the Hanze University of Applied Science in Groningen, the Netherlands. Previously, I studied English Translation in Homs University for three years, in 2009 I started my online translation business (Victoria LTS) and developed my skills as a video subtitler and worked as freelance subtitler for 5 years.

Even though subtitling was my career for years, I'm eager these days to build and develop my ICT skills and be prepared for my soon-future career as a project manager. Studying at Hanze is giving me the nessceriy skills to land in a career confidently. You can read more about projects, subjects and skills I learnt at my university so far in: Skills.

Subtitling Services

My work was outsourced to several companines such as: Netflix and SONY, I also worked for different individual clients such as: EasyDaf, and have in my account more than 30.000 minutes subtitled in 5 languages (English, Arabic, Dutch, German and Spanish). Beside subtitling services, I also trained nearly 100 Arabic-speaking students on subtitling via ATIDA.

My subtitling services cover all subtitling aspects, starting from transcribing, translation, timecoding, proofreading and embedding. I also offer customized subtitle service such as karoke, captionning and SLS (Same-language subtitle). High quality is always guarnted.
